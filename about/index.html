About Me

I am currently studying statistical analysis and programming at the London School of Economics and Political Science in the MSc Health Data Science programme. I completed my BS in Statistics with a minor in Geographic Information Science from the University of Connecticut in May 2020. I have experience in data analytics and visualization, meta-analysis, biostatistics, time series analysis, spatial analysis, as well strong technical skills in R, SAS, SPSS and GIS. This year, I hope to gain practical experience in Python through my coursework and participation in the LSESU Data Science Society. In the near future, I plan to pursue a career in statistical consulting and am looking forward to working on a variety of analysis projects.

In my free time, I enjoy exploring London, hiking, rowing, fencing and studying the Czech language.
